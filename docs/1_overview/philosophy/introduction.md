# Introduction

Philosophy is the study of fundamental questions about existence, knowledge, values, reason, mind, and language. Here is a high-level overview of the major schools of thought covered in this knowledge bank.

## Schools of Thought

- **[[absurdism|Absurdism]]**: Laughing at the silence of the universe.
  - *Concept*: Seeking meaning in a meaningless world is funny, not sad.
- **[[cynicism|Cynicism]]**: Living like a dog to be truly free.
  - *Concept*: Rejecting social norms (wealth, power, fame) to live in virtue with nature.
- **[[existentialism|Existentialism]]**: You contain the paintbrush, not just the canvas.
  - *Concept*: Existence precedes essence; you are not born with a purpose, you must create one.
- **[[hedonism|Hedonism]]**: Pleasure is the only compass.
  - *Concept*: The pursuit of pleasure and avoidance of pain is the highest good.
- **[[law-of-attraction|Law of Attraction]]**: Like attracts like.
  - *Concept*: The belief that positive thoughts bring positive results into a person's life.
- **[[nihilism|Nihilism]]**: The game has no rules and no winner.
  - *Concept*: Life is without objective meaning, purpose, or intrinsic value.
- **[[paradox|Paradox]]**: A truth that sounds like a lie.
  - *Concept*: A statement that contradicts itself but might still be true.
- **[[pragmatism|Pragmatism]]**: If it works, it's true.
  - *Concept*: Assessing theories or beliefs in terms of the success of their practical application.
- **[[stoicism|Stoicism]]**: You cannot control the storm, only the ship.
  - *Concept*: Happiness is found in accepting the moment as it presents itself, not in our desire to control it.
- **[[utilitarianism|Utilitarianism]]**: The greatest good for the greatest number.
  - *Concept*: The belief that the best action is the one that maximizes happiness and minimizes suffering.
