# Blue Team

The castle guards.

The **Blue Team** is the internal security staff responsible for defending the organization. They are the ones watching the monitors, installing the [[firewalls|Firewalls]], patching the software, and responding when an alarm goes off.

## Responsibilities

*   **monitoring:** Watching logs for suspicious activity ([[soc|SOC]]).
*   **Incident Response:** Kicking the hackers out when a breach happens.
*   **Hardening:** Making the servers tougher to crack.

## The endless job

The [[red-team|Red Team]] only needs to find *one* open window to win. The Blue Team must close *every* window to win.
