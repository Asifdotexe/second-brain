# Cybersecurity Frameworks & Teams

The structures and teams that govern modern digital defense.

**Note**: This section focuses specifically on defensive and offensive security units. For broader concepts like Risk Management, see **[[foundations|Foundations]]**. For regulations like GDPR, see **[[governance|Governance]]**.

## The Catalog of Frameworks & Teams

### 1. [[blue-team|Blue Team]]
The "castle guards": internal security staff responsible for defending the network.

### 2. [[red-team|Red Team]]
The "adversaries": security professionals who act as attackers to test resilience.

### 3. [[purple-team|Purple Team]]
The "coaches": a collaborative unit where Red and Blue teams share feedback.

### 4. [[soc|SOC (Security Operations Center)]]
Mission Control for cyber defense: a 24/7/365 command center for monitoring logs.
