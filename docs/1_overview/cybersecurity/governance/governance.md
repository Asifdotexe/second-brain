# Governance & Compliance

Laws, standards, and the ethics of security.

Cybersecurity is governed by a complex web of legal requirements, ethical standards, and industry-specific regulations. This section covers the "rules of the road" for managing sensitive data and maintaining professional integrity.

## The Catalog of Governance

### 1. [[gdpr|GDPR]]
The European Union's comprehensive data privacy regulation that has become a global standard.

### 2. [[hipaa|HIPAA]]
The U.S. standard for protecting sensitive patient health information.

### 3. [[pci-dss|PCI DSS]]
The security standard for organizations that handle credit card and payment data.

### 4. [[security-ethics|Security Ethics & Privacy]]
The moral guidelines and professional obligations of security experts.

### 5. [[cisa|CISA]]
The U.S. federal agency responsible for national cyber risk and critical infrastructure protection.

### 6. [[cve|CVE & CVSS]]
The standardized systems for naming and scoring known security vulnerabilities.

### 7. [[bug-bounty|Bug Bounty Programs]]
Crowdsourced security initiatives used to find and disclose vulnerabilities responsibly.
