### India doubles down on state-backed venture capital, approving $1.1B fund

![india](https://techcrunch.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/india-flag.jpg?resize=1280,853)

Date: 14 February 2026

- The program is structured as a fund of funds, a common venture capital model.

### Robot Dogs Are on Going on Patrol at the 2026 World Cup in Mexico

![robot-dogs](https://media.wired.com/photos/698f7d2569fc972689ff804e/3:2/w_1920,c_limit/whatsapp-image-2026-02-12-at-12.45.29.jpeg)

Date: 14 February 2026

- Authorities in Mexico’s Guadalupe, Nuevo León, this week unveiled four robot dogs that will be part of the security devices at BBVA Stadium.
- The “K9-X” unit functions as a kind of first responder only.
- The robot dogs are not armed, but each unit incorporates video cameras, night vision, and communication systems.
